## Inactivity

One of the most common reasons for lush leaving a server is inactivty, which can occur when lush hasn't been interacted with for a prolonged period of time. You can reinvite lowest by clicking [here](/invite). Your previous settings will be retained.

## Blacklisted

If your server is caught violating Discord's & lush's Terms of Service, it will be blacklisted, resulting in lush leaving your server. Make sure to review the rules and guidelines to ensure compliance. If you believe your server was wrongfully blacklisted, please contact [support](/discord).
